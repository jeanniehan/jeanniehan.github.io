Ephemeral Flower Collection

Ghost Orchid

A flower foreign to its culture, the Ghost Orchid is a flower that flourishes in the dark. It's native to Florida, and blooms once a year between June and October.

"Now she begins to open, expand to find a new language beyond loneliness and grief." - Sydney Lea

Morning Glory

The Morning Glory, often known as the Moonflower, blooms once a year.

"Morning glory lunar flower best for entertaining in dark hours. Just wait first bloom will appear then jealousy comes from those flowers." - Sara Kendrick

The Kadupul Flower

The Kadupul flower is a cactus blossom that rarely blooms. It blooms during the night then withers during the day, so you can only have a glimpse of this blossom a few hours during the night. It requires special growing conditions, so in retrospect, these flowers are best seen in the imagination.
